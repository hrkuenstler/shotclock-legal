Underwater Hockey Tools

ShotClock & ScoreBoard

Apps for timekeeping and scoring in underwater hockey, talking to ESP32-based hardware over a local MQTT network.

The Apps

ShotClock

Per-team shot clock for underwater hockey matches. Controls hardware displays via MQTT.

ScoreBoard

Score, period timer, and buzzer control for match displays. Pairs with ShotClock over the same MQTT broker.
